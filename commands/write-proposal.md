# /write-proposal

Write a client-winning freelance proposal.

## Instructions

1. Ask the user to paste the job posting, client brief, or intake call notes.
2. Ask what their relevant experience is for this specific project.
3. Ask for their preferred pricing (fixed, hourly, or range).
4. Follow the proposal-writer skill structure to generate the proposal:
   - Hook that references their specific problem
   - Diagnosis showing you understood the brief
   - Proposed approach with specific action verbs
   - Concrete deliverables with quantities
   - Timeline with phases and check-in points
   - Investment section with value framing
   - Brief "Why Me" with a specific result
   - Clear call to action
5. Deliver the proposal as clean, copy-paste-ready text.
6. Offer to create a Word document or adjust tone/pricing.
