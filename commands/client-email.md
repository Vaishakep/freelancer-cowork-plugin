# /freelancer:client-email

Draft a professional client-facing email for any situation.

## Instructions

1. Ask the user what kind of email they need to write:
   - Project update
   - Scope change response (client asked for more than agreed)
   - Deadline extension request
   - Requesting feedback on deliverables
   - Requesting a testimonial
   - Following up on a proposal
   - Delivering bad news
   - Re-engaging a past client
2. Ask for the key details: client name, project context, and the specific situation.
3. Ask about the relationship tone: new client, established, corporate, startup/creative.
4. Follow the **client-comms** skill's templates and tone calibration for the specific message type.
5. Generate the email ready to send, keeping it under 200 words unless complexity demands more.
6. Offer: "Want me to adjust the tone?", "Should I draft a follow-up if they don't respond?", or "Want a shorter/longer version?"
