# /freelancer:chase-payment

Draft a payment follow-up email calibrated to how overdue the invoice is.

## Instructions

1. Ask: How many days overdue is the invoice?
2. Ask: What's the invoice number and amount?
3. Ask: What's the relationship like with this client? (new, established, difficult)
4. Load the invoice-followup skill and select the appropriate escalation level.
5. Generate the email ready to send.
6. Offer to draft the next escalation level as a backup.
