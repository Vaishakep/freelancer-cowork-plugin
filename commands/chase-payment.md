# /chase-payment

Draft a payment follow-up email calibrated to how overdue the invoice is.

## Instructions

1. Ask: How many days overdue is the invoice?
2. Ask: What's the invoice number and amount?
3. Ask: What's the relationship like with this client? (new, established, difficult)
4. Select the appropriate escalation level from the invoice-followup skill:
   - Level 1 (1-3 days): Friendly nudge, assumes oversight
   - Level 2 (7-10 days): Warm but direct, references project value
   - Level 3 (14-21 days): Professional, boundary-setting, mentions pausing work
   - Level 4 (30+ days): Formal demand with deadline and next steps
5. Generate the email ready to send.
6. Offer to draft the next escalation level as a backup.
