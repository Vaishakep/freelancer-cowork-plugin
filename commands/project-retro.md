# /project-retro

Run a full retrospective on a completed project. Generates three outputs: lessons document, case study draft, and pricing intelligence.

## Instructions

1. Ask the user about the project: client, deliverables, timeline, what they charged, hours spent.
2. Ask what went well and what was challenging.
3. Generate all three retrospective outputs:
   - **Lessons document**: What went well, what didn't, scope accuracy, time accuracy, effective hourly rate
   - **Case study draft**: Result-focused headline, challenge, approach, results, draft testimonial
   - **Pricing intelligence**: Quoted vs. actual, effective rate vs. target rate, verdict and recommendation
4. Offer to format the case study for LinkedIn or their portfolio site.
5. Offer to draft a testimonial request email to the client.
