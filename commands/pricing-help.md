# /freelancer:pricing-help

Get strategic help with freelance pricing and rate negotiations.

## Instructions

1. Ask the user what they need help with:
   - Setting rates for a new service
   - Responding to a pricing objection from a client
   - Raising rates with an existing client
   - Choosing between hourly, project, or value-based pricing
2. Follow the **rate-negotiation** skill's guidance for their specific scenario.
3. Walk through the relevant section based on their situation.
4. If they're responding to an objection, ask them to share the client's exact words so the response is tailored.
5. Deliver actionable language they can use in conversation or email.
6. Offer follow-up help: "Want me to draft a follow-up if the client pushes back?", "Should I help scope a reduced version that fits their budget?", or "Want me to calculate your minimum viable rate for this type of work?"
